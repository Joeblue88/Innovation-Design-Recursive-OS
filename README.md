# Innovation Design Recursive OS (ID-ROS)

An AI-native strategic framework for recursive business and design innovation. ID-ROS transforms linear project management into a systemic, feedback-driven operation.

## Core Philosophy

- **Process as the Spine:** Adaptive leadership monitors the health of the innovation flow.
- **Future as the Compass:** Strategic foresight defines the horizon of possibilities.
- **Human as the Tension:** Deep empathy reframes business KPIs into human needs.
- **Machine as the Proof:** Emerging technology validates hypotheses through agile making.

## The Five Pillars

1. Adaptive Leadership (The Spine): Monitors team dynamics, psychological safety, and the "Process" health of a project.
2. Strategic Foresight (The Compass): Navigates long-term uncertainty using the Future Cone and Causal Layered Analysis (CLA).
3. Venture Transformation (The Engine): Generates strategic urgency and builds a compelling Case for Change to disrupt the status quo.
4. Human-Centric Innovation (The Heart): Reframes commercial KPIs into deep human tensions, ensuring ethical stewardship.
5. Emerging Tech Strategy (The Proof): Validates hypotheses through Agile Making and "thinking with hands."


## How to Use

1. Upload the YAML files in `/skills` to your AI agent's knowledge base.
2. Load the `meta-orchestrator.yaml` as the primary instruction set.
3. Initiate sessions with a "Leadership Check-in" to align team dynamics and goals.

## How it Works: The Recursive Loop

1. Unlike linear workflows, ID-ROS encourages back-and-forth movement:
2. Foresight informs the Venture Strategy.
3. Venture Strategy identifies the Human Tension.
4. Human Tension guides the Tech Prototype.
5. Tech Data refines the Future Foresight.
6. Leadership manages the entire flow to prevent stagnation.
